Tankpilot — Privacy Policy

Last updated: 1 July 2026

Tankpilot helps you find the nearest and cheapest fuel stations in Belgium and estimates your remaining fuel and driving range. This policy explains what data the app uses and how. In short: your data stays on your device. Nothing is uploaded, sold, or shared.

1. Information we use

  • Location (GPS). Used to show nearby stations, measure the distance you drive, and estimate your range. With your explicit consent, location may be used in the background (when the app is closed or not in use) to automatically detect trips and alert you to cheaper stations ahead.
  • Vehicle & fuel details you enter (tank size, average consumption, fuel type, current fuel level, litres added).
  • Prices you report for stations, and learned consumption figures.

2. How your data is stored

All of the above is stored only on your device (in local app storage). It is never transmitted to us or to any third party. We have no servers and no user accounts. Uninstalling the app removes this data.

3. Background location

Background location is off by default. It is only used if you turn on "Auto-track trips" and accept the in-app disclosure. It powers automatic trip detection and low-fuel alerts. You can turn it off at any time in the app or in your device settings.

4. Third-party services

  • OpenStreetMap (Overpass API). The app queries for fuel-station locations near you. Your approximate coordinates are sent to the Overpass service to return nearby stations. See the OSM Foundation privacy policy.
  • Google Maps. Tapping "Navigate" opens Google Maps with the station's coordinates. Google's use of that data is governed by Google's privacy policy.

5. Data we do not collect

No advertising identifiers, no analytics, no tracking, no contacts, no accounts.

6. Children

Tankpilot is not directed at children under 13.

7. Changes

We may update this policy; the "last updated" date above will change accordingly.
